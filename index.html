The Biodesign Lab

Rice University, Keck 301

Our research spans the boundary between theoretical and experimental synthetic biology. We use an interdisciplinary approach to 1) uncover the underlying design principles governing gene networks and microbial consortia, 2) develop new mathematical tools to better describe gene networks, and 3) engineer novel synthetic gene circuits for environmental and medical applications.
